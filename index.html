About Me

What separates me from other classic defensive midfielders is a high soccer IQ, ability to break lines, and skill at creating turnovers while maintaining possession. As the focal point of Iowa and Boston's build-out, I have been very reliable in possession and a high-volume/efficiency passer with a good forward pass accuracy rating using both feet.

I pride myself on finding line-breaking passes or breaking through on the dribble. I believe that intercepting passes is one of my greatest strengths. It demonstrates my tactical IQ and ability to read the game.
